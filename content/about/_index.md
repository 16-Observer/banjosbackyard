Banjo's Backyard Farm is a small permaculture homestead near Monroe, WA. We raise happy pastured hens for fresh eggs and grow seasonal vegetables, berry shrubs, and young trees. This site is run by one person — easy pickup by appointment (8am–8pm daily) or arrange delivery.

Our approach: soil health, diverse plantings, and minimal chemical inputs. Follow our updates on the Blog and see current offerings on the Products page.

Location: Off State Route 203 — see the Contact page for a map and quickest contact methods.
